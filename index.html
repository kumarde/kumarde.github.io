I am a postdoctoral researcher at Stanford University. My research focuses on online abuse – like hate and harassment and misinformation – from the lens of computer security and privacy. I use large-scale measurements to measure how abuse is operationalized and design defenses to better protect Internet users. You can find my publications here, my CV here, and my Google Scholar page here. I am the recipient of the 2020 CRA/CCC CI Fellowship and the 2022 David and Helen Gurley Brown Institute Magic Grant.
